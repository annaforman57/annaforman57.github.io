    • Dogs

    A dog is a neccesity if you're in need for a friend that loves you unconditionally. Dogs are the most popular choice of pet in the U.S and are an excellent choice for those looking for a companion. Dogs are for those who are adventerous, and like the outdoors. Consider adopting at your local shelter today!

    Interesting Facts

  • Petting your dog for 15-30 minutes lowers your blood pressure and reduces stress by lowering the level of cortisol (a stress hormone) in your blood.
  • Their sense of smell is at least 40x better than ours
  • Dogs possess as many as 300 millionreceptors in their nose
  • 3 dogs survived the Titanic sinking

    • Cats

    There are over 500 million domestic cats in the world. Cat are very playful, but also sensitive when it comes to their personal space. A cat would be a great choice of pet for those who are independent, and want a low maintenece pet.

    Interesting Facts

  • Cats can jump up to 6 times their height.
  • There are roughly 370 million pet cats in th world

    • Birds

    Birds are quiet, shy, and harmless yet display intelligence. They can identify their keepers and know when it's feeding time. Turtles and tortoises are very sensitive to loud noises, vibrations, and sudden bright lights. Those who are hard-working, reliable, and easygoing would benefit from having a pet turtle.

    Interesting Facts

  • Birds Are Literally Dinosaurs
  • You can get a pet bird for as little as $10 for small birds like Parakeets, or as much as $5,000+ for large, exotic birds, like some types of Macaws.

    • Bunnies

    Those who are creative and outgoing would enjoy having a bunny as a companion. Bunnies are silly, yet timid and high spirited.

    Interesting Facts

  • To express happiness, bunnies will sometimes jump around and flick their heads and feet
  • A baby rabbit is called a kit, a female is called a doe and a male is called a buck.
